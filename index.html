About Curious Fox

The indecisive person's guide to entertainment...

Spending hours searching for a film or a new podcast? That was us. We love finding new content, but sometimes it takes a long time to find it. And sometimes you don't even know where to start looking. That's why we created Curious Fox. For when you want something new but don't want to spend all of your time looking for it. We have done all the hard work for you by finding the best for you so you can enjoy, grow and learn. There's a mix of new and classic, and many more for you to explore.

Choose Media if you want to find a new TV show, book, podcast, film or social media platform. You can also choose a Genre like fantasy, technology, gardening or beauty if you know what kind of topic you are interested in. If you are indecisive (like us) then use the Pick for me button and let us choose! We love to connect with people and find new content, so use our Connect page to share your own work with us, or recommend something you have seen.

One of our favourite genres at the moment is 'Eco'. We've been finding the best eco-friendly content including podcasts, TikToks and Instagrams. Each week we'll pick something to share with you in our featured pick below!

Our Recommended Fantasy Stories

For stories of magic, the supernatural and illusion, look no further. Here we provide out best recommendations of where to get your next out of this world fix with the best fantasy books, movies, TV shows and podcasts.

Top Picks

  • The Best Fantasy Books:

    • If you're looking to immerse yourself then The Lord of the Rings and The Song of Ice and Fire Series are always a guarnteed bet. But if you're looking for something new then consider the Demon Child Trilogy, or the Mist Born Trilogy.

  • The Best Fantasy TV Shows:

    • Fantasy TV has a wide range of stories to keep you entertained, for a more feel good show consider Good Omens starring one of the best on screen friendships or Once Upon a Time. For a darker twist on the genre, however we would recommend the world of Penny Dreadful or Stranger Things

  • Best Fantasy Movies:

    • Our recommendations for fantasy movies includes the latest additions to the Marvel Cinematic Universe- Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness and Thor: Love and Thunder. For a comedy take on a fantasy show then Free Guy offers a comedy film exploring friendships and reality.
